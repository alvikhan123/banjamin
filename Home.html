What inspires me

I grew up in the suburbs of Phoenix, Arizona, where my parents struggled to put food on the table and worked relentlessley day in and day out. As a child, my parents enforced the idea of me going to college, getting a good job, the whole nine, as they wanted what's best for me; but I knew that's not what was best for me.

In my 2nd year of attending Michigan State University, I knew becoming an entrepenuer was my true calling. I dropped out with both my parent's blessings and started my business journey. After launching multiple companies and designing clothes for people around the world, I have moved to sharing my expertise and experience with upcoming entrepenuers to help them launch their own businesses and consult them to a new way of living outside of the standard.

My goal is to aid 10,000 entrepenuers into starting their own business into a successful business that can revolutionize the world.
